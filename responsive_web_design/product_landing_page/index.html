Why choose John's?

At John's we understand your need for a product that goes beyond your expectations in all areas. That is why all our jets go through a lengthy selection process. Read how all our jets adhere to our company values.

Certified

All jets are certified as original from the manufacturer, and their complete nothing-left-out flight record come standard with every jet, giving you total knownledge and security.

Serviced

Any jet that has anything not done in accordance with manufacturer service recommendations is instantly disqualified to be sold through John's. With us, you know what you're getting, and in which condition.

Proven

Every plane we buy has proven itself through difficult conditions, conditions that is has been built to handle. This is because we believe that when the product lives up to its own standard we can fascilite your new jet ownership with a confidence in its future success.

In stock

Our mission is to always have a jet ready for every customer, no matter who it is. That is why every collection is diverse. Once a plane is united with a new owner, we will work diligently to replace the missing offer type as soon as possible.
